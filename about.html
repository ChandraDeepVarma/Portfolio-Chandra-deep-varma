about me

Hello! I’m Chandra Deep Varma Namburi, and I graduated with a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Nadimpalli Satyanarayana Raju Institute of Technology (NSRIT) in Visakhapatnam in 2022. While my academic background is in engineering, I have developed a strong passion for the IT field, which led me to pursue further education at NXT Wave.

I have gained skills in various programming languages and tools, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React.js, Node.js, and data analytics tools like Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and ETL frameworks. This diverse skill set equips me to tackle challenges in both web development and data analysis.

I am an enthusiastic learner who enjoys exploring new technologies and trends. In my free time, I indulge in web surfing and stay updated on the latest advancements in the tech industry. Additionally, I have a creative side; I love playing the guitar, playing cricket with friends, and listening to music.

As I embark on my career journey, I am actively seeking opportunities that will allow me to apply my skills and knowledge while contributing to innovative projects.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I invite you to explore my work and connect with me if you’re interested in collaboration or if you have any opportunities that align with my skills.
